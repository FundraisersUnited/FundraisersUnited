Who We Are

Fundraisers United is a specialist face-to-face fundraising agency operating throughout Australia. We connect Australian charities with passionate, long-term donors by deploying highly trained, internationally recruited F2F fundraisers.

Our mission is to deliver sustained success for our clients and rewarding careers for our team through rigorous performance management, a focus on validation and donor retention, competitive staff packages, ethical practices, and a seamless international transition process.

Our Values

  • Performance Excellence: Driven by achieving and exceeding key metrics
  • Quality & Integrity: Commitment to high-quality donor acquisition and ethical practices
  • Staff Development & Retention: Investing in our team's success and wellbeing
  • Partnership: Building long-term, collaborative relationships
  • Accountability: Taking ownership of performance targets and commitments

Our Approach

International Expertise

We source experienced fundraising talent from international markets, ensuring high-quality performance from day one. Our fundraisers bring proven expertise and a fresh perspective to Australian campaigns.

Quality-Focused

Our performance systems are designed to prioritize quality over quantity. We incentivize our fundraisers to secure validated, higher-value donors who stay committed to your cause long-term.

Structured Management

With a 1:4 team leader to fundraiser ratio, we provide close supervision, coaching, and performance management to ensure consistent results across all campaigns.

Staff Retention

Our unique international transition program ensures continuity of expertise and operational capacity, addressing one of the biggest challenges in F2F fundraising – staff turnover.

Dignity in Fundraising

How Dignified Fundraisers Lead to Quality Donors

At Fundraisers United, we believe that treating our fundraisers with dignity and respect directly translates to the quality of donors they attract. When fundraisers feel valued, supported, and properly compensated, they bring their best selves to every donor interaction.

Our approach is simple yet revolutionary in the fundraising industry: we prioritize the wellbeing of our people because we know it directly impacts our results. Unlike traditional models that often lead to burnout and high turnover, our dignity-first approach creates sustainable careers for fundraisers and exceptional value for charities.

Higher Quality Donors

Fundraisers who feel respected engage authentically with prospects, resulting in donors who truly connect with causes and remain committed long-term.

Reduced Turnover

By treating employees the way they deserve, we bring fundraising instability to a standstill, eliminating the constant recruitment and training cycle.

Authentic Relationships

Dignified fundraisers build genuine relationships rather than pursuing short-term metrics, resulting in donors who feel truly connected to the cause.

Sustainable Growth

Our model creates a virtuous cycle where satisfied employees attract committed donors, leading to predictable, long-term revenue growth for charities.

"When we invest in our fundraisers' wellbeing and dignity, we're ultimately investing in the quality and longevity of donor relationships. This isn't just the right thing to do—it's the smart approach to sustainable fundraising."

— Fundraisers United Leadership Team

Join Our Team

Are you an experienced face-to-face fundraiser looking for your next adventure? Fundraisers United offers competitive packages, supportive team environments, and the opportunity to work in beautiful Australia while advancing your fundraising career.

Creating a dignified environment for fundraisers has been lacking globally. We're changing that by offering stable employment and treating fundraisers with the respect they deserve for the important work they do.

We Offer:

  • Permanent Employment Model: Moving away from casual contracts to fixed and permanent positions with stability
  • Competitive Base Wages: From AUD $35-45/hour depending on role, plus performance bonuses
  • Structured Incentives: Weekly bonuses based on validated sign-ups and retention
  • Genuine Benefits: Including 4 weeks annual leave, 10 days personal leave, and 11% superannuation
  • Comprehensive Relocation Package: Covering flights and initial accommodation
  • Working Holiday Visa Support: Specialized management and transition assistance
  • Career Development: Clear progression pathways from Fundraiser to Team Leader to Manager roles
  • Retention Rewards: Additional bonuses after successful completion of 6 months

Our approach isn't just better for our team—it delivers superior results for our charity partners through stable, motivated fundraisers who build authentic connections with donors.
